Business Analysis Portfolio Project - Increase Sales for an E-commerce Company
Portfolio Project: "Increase Sales for an E-commerce Company"
Scenario: You are a BA at a Swedish e-commerce company. The manager says: "We are losing customers and don't know why. We want to increase sales by 20% in 6 months."

Part 1 — Problem Identification
Problem: The company has lost 20% of its customers in the last 6 months.
As-Is:

Fewer customers today compared to 6 months ago
Customers do not return despite discount offers via email and SMS
Delivery time is perceived as too slow
Return process is complicated

Data:

A customer survey shows dissatisfaction with delivery time, products not matching descriptions, and a difficult return process

Root Cause Analysis:

Long delivery time → customers choose competitors
Products don't match descriptions → dissatisfaction and returns
Complicated return process → customers don't come back

As-To-Be:

Satisfied and returning customers
Delivery within 3 days
Products matching descriptions
Simple return process
20% increase in sales within 6 months


Part 2 — Stakeholders

Customers, employees, suppliers, manager

Interview Questions:

What do you think is the cause of the problem?
What would improve your experience?
What would an ideal solution look like?


Part 3 — Requirements
BR: Increase sales by 20% within 6 months by improving the customer experience.
FR:

The system shall send a personalized SMS/email within 20 days after purchase with relevant product offers
The system shall offer free returns on the first purchase and include a return label in the package
The system shall guarantee delivery within max 3 days

NFR:

SMS/email shall be sent automatically within 2 seconds after trigger
The system shall be available 99.9% of the time


Part 4 — User Stories

"As a customer, I want delivery within 3 days so that I can trust the company"
"As a customer, I want a simple return process so that I feel confident shopping again"
"As a customer, I want personalized offers so that I can find products I like"
"As a manager, I want to see returning customers increase by 20% so that sales grow"

Part 7 — Recommendations
To increase sales by 20%, three actions are required:

Delivery time — Guarantee delivery within 3 days. Customers choose competitors when delivery is too slow.
Product quality — Ensure products match their descriptions to reduce returns and build trust.
Return process — Offer a return label in the package and free returns on the first purchase to lower the barrier to shop.
